Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I Login?
To log in to the Tripmartz Corporate Employee portal, follow these steps:
  • You have to contact your Administrator or Travel desk department to obtain User ID and Password.
  • Once you receive ID and Password, Please Visit the Tripmartz Corporate Employee portal login page.(URL)
  • Enter your registered email ID and password and Login ID.
  • Click Login.
2. How do I reset my password for the Corporate Employee portal?
If you forgot your password, follow these steps:
  • Go to the Corporate Employee portal login page.
  • Click on the Forgot Password?
  • Enter your registered email ID and click Submit.
  • You will receive an email with a password reset link. Click on it.
  • Set a new password and confirm it.
  • Log in with your new password.

4. How do I update my profile details?
To update your profile details:
  • Log in to the Corporate Employee portal.
  • Go to the Profile section.
  • Click Edit Profile and update your details.
  • Click Save Changes to confirm.
5. To book tickets:
  • Log in to the Tripmartz Corporate Employee portal.
  • Select the category: Flights, Hotels.
  • Enter travel details (destination, dates, passengers, etc.).
  • Click Search and choose your preferred option.
  • Complete the booking.
  • You will receive a confirmation email and booking reference number.
6. Can I do my personal bookings in the portal?
To Book for Personal Travel?
  • Log in to the Tripmartz Corporate Employee portal.
  • Choose the booking category (Flights, Hotels,etc.).
  • Select "Personal" as the travel type.
  • Enter travel details (destination, date, passenger info).
  • Select a suitable option from the available choices.
  • Fill in passenger/guest details.
  • Choose your payment option and confirm the booking.
  • Receive booking confirmation via email/SMS.
  • Manage your booking from the "REPORTS" section.
7. Can I modify or cancel my booking?
  • Hotels: Some hotels offer free cancellations, while others may have a cancellation fee.
  • Flights: Airlines have specific rules for modifications and cancellations, which may involve fees.
  • Other Services: For any other bookings, please refer to the cancellation policy mentioned during booking.
  • You can directly click on the cancel button for the cancellation and your booking will be cancelled.
8. How do I add special requests (meals, seat preferences, wheelchair assistance)?
You can request special services while booking:
  • At the time of booking you will be given the option to add SSR services (Seats, extra baggage, Meals and all) at the time of Booking.
  • Or else, You can contact our support team to add requests manually.
9. How do I check my booking status?
To check the status of your booking:
  • Log in to the Tripmartz Corporate Employee portal.
  • Go to REPORTS.
  • Select the booking reference number to view status (Confirmed, Pending, or Canceled)
10. Will I get a full refund if I cancel my trip?
Refunds depend on the cancellation policy:
  • If canceled within the free cancellation period, you may get a full refund.
  • Some bookings may have partial refunds based on airline/hotel rules.
  • Refunds may take 5-7 business days to process.

12. Are there cancellation charges for changing travel plans?
Yes, cancellation charges vary based on the airline, hotel, or package provider:
  • Some bookings may allow free cancellations within a time frame.
  • Others may have charges based on the cancellation date.
  • Check the cancellation policy before booking.
