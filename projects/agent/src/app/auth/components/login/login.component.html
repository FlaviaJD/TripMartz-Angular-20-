1. What is a Travel Agent?
A travel agent is a professional who assists individual travellers or groups of travellers with travel planning and arrangements. These include flights, hotels and buses. Many travel agents all over world join with a B2B travel portal and become their travel partners to get all the travel arrangements done for their customers.
